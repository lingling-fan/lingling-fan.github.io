Lingling Fan (范玲玲)

I am now working as a Research Scholarship Block Postdoctoral Fellow with Prof. Liu Yang in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from East China Normal University, China, in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Lihua Xu (NYU Shanghai). I had been a Research Assistant in Cyber Security Lab of NTU (2017-2019).

My research interests are but not limited to Software Security Analysis, Software Testing and Analysis, and Big Data-driven Analysis.

I will join Nankai University as an Associate Professor (100 Young Academic Leaders of Nankai University).

Email: llfan@ntu.edu.sg
